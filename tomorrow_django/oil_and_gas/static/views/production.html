For production data $p_i$ and a fit $f(t)$ using data up to $t_{end}$, the error on future total production is: $$error = {\left( \int_{t_{end}}^{t_{now}} f(t)dt \right) - \sum_{i=t_{end}}^{t_{now}} p_i \over \sum_{i=t_{end}}^{t_{now}} p_i}.$$
